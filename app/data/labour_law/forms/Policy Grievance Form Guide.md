# Guide: Policy Grievance Form

## Purpose
Use this form for disputes involving the *interpretation* or general application of the agreement that affects the entire bargaining unit or a large class of employees.

## Key Difference
A Policy Grievance skips Step 1 and is usually filed directly at Step 2 or Step 3 by the Union (see Article 8.11).

## Content
- **Statement**: Focus on the Employer's "general policy" or "interpretation" rather than specific individual facts.
