# 🔍 BCGEU Navigator

Welcome to the **BCGEU Navigator**, your professional AI companion for labor relations and collective agreement analysis.

### 🚀 Getting Started
1. **Choose your Persona**: Click the profile selector in the top-left (e.g., 🔍 **Lookup**, ⚖️ **Grieve**, or 📋 **Manage**) to tailor the AI's perspective.
2. **Access Documents**: Click the ⚙️ **Settings** icon in the chat bar and use the **Document Navigator** dropdown to instantly view and download collective agreements.
3. **Ask a Question**: Use the sample questions below or type your own query.

### 📚 Features
- **Intelligent RAG**: Context-aware answers based on actual collective agreement text.
- **Persona Routing**: Tailored advice for stewards, members, and management.
- **Deep Linking**: Reference specific articles and pages in your queries.

*Disclaimer: This is an AI-powered tool. Always verify with the official collective agreement text for legal or grievance purposes.*
