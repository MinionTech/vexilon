### MILLHAVEN AUDIT CRITERIA
To justify discipline for off-duty conduct, management must demonstrate ONE of these five factors:

1. REPUTATIONAL HARM: Conduct harms (or potentially harms) the employer's reputation or brand.
2. INABILITY TO PERFORM: Conduct renders the employee unable to perform their job satisfactorily.
3. CO-WORKER IMPACT: Behaviour leads to other employees' refusal or reluctance to work with the member.
4. CRIMINAL BREACH: Serious breach of the Criminal Code injurious to the employer's reputation.
5. OPERATIONAL INTERFERENCE: Conduct creates difficulty for the employer in efficiently managing the business.
